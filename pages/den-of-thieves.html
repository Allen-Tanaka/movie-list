Den of Thieves (2018)

Directed by Christian Gudegast

About the Film

Den of Thieves is a gritty crime thriller set in Los Angeles. It follows an elite unit of the LA County Sheriff's Department as they attempt to track down and stop a crew of highly trained, ex-military bank robbers who are planning to pull off an audacious heist on the Federal Reserve Bank. As the two sides circle each other, the line between law and crime begins to blur.

Why I Love It

Den of Thieves does not get nearly enough credit. It is tense, well-paced, and full of strong performances — particularly from Gerard Butler as the rough-around-the-edges detective Big Nick, and Pablo Schreiber as the cold, calculating crew leader Merrimen.

What sets it apart is how it treats both sides as capable and intelligent. You are never quite sure who to root for, and the final act delivers a twist that reframes everything you thought you understood about the story. It rewards attention.

Key Details

  • Release year: 2018
  • Genre: Action / Crime / Thriller
  • Runtime: 140 minutes
  • Starring: Gerard Butler, Pablo Schreiber, O'Shea Jackson Jr., 50 Cent
  • My rating: 9/10
